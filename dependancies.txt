That's genuinely all you need. Both are pure Python, no system libs required on Linux... except one thing 
— pyautogui on Linux needs a display backend:

And if you're on Wayland (Pop!_OS 22.04+ defaults to it), pynput and pyautogui both need X11 compatibility layer:
bash# check if you're on Wayland or X11
echo $XDG_SESSION_TYPE

If it says wayland, run the app like this:
bashDISPLAY=:0 python3 main.py

Or force X11 session in Pop!_OS — on the login screen, click the gear icon bottom-right and pick GNOME on Xorg.
